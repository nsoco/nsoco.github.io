What's PC Bottleneck?

PC Bottleneck is typically very overlooked.

It's a result of either the GPU or CPU being too weak for the other.

This results in the system not performing not as well as it should be. However, it is very easy to fix.

For example, having a GPU that is very strong, like an RTX 5090 paired with a weak CPU will result in the full potential of the GPU not being used.

How can I fix PC Bottleneck?

A common way to fix PC bottleneck is to check other websites that can calculate PC bottleneck. The website can provide info on the percent of bottleneck, and which component is bottlenecking the other.
